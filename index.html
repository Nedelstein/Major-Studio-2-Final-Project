MS2 Final Project

This is an interactive audio/visual story about a true incident. It is retold by the four different people who experienced the event.

The story is separated into six chapters. To listen, hover over the icons with your mouse. When you are done with one, you may scroll down to the next chapter, or listen to another perspective in the same one.

Headphones are recommended.
